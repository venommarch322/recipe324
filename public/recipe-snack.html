Crispy Roasted Chickpeas

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chickpeas
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss chickpeas with oil and spices.
  3. Spread on a baking tray.
  4. Roast until crispy and golden.
