Chicken Avocado Wrap

Ingredients

  • 2 tortillas
  • 1 cup cooked chicken
  • 1 avocado
  • 1/2 cup lettuce
  • 1/4 cup tomato
  • 2 tablespoons mayo

Instructions

  1. Warm the tortillas.
  2. Layer chicken, avocado, lettuce, and tomato.
  3. Add mayo and roll tightly.
  4. Cut in half and serve.
