Fresh Veggie Bowl

A simple, healthy, and colorful veggie bowl recipe perfect for lunch or a light dinner.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked quinoa
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup cooked chickpeas
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Add quinoa to two serving bowls as the base.
  2. Arrange tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, and chickpeas on top.
  3. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice over the bowls.
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then serve immediately.

Cooking Tips

You can add grilled chicken, tofu, feta, or seeds for extra flavor and protein. This recipe is flexible and easy to customize.
