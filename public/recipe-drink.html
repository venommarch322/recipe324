Fresh Lemon Mint Cooler

Ingredients

  • 2 lemons
  • 6 mint leaves
  • 2 cups cold water
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • Ice cubes

Instructions

  1. Squeeze the lemons into a jug.
  2. Add mint, honey, and cold water.
  3. Stir well and add ice.
  4. Serve chilled.
